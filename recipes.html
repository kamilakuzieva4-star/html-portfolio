Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Italian pasta with eggs, cheese, and meat(bacon)

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Sausage
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water
  2. Cook the sausage
  3. Mix eggs with cheese
  4. Combine everything
  5. Add pepper

Pancakes

Description

Soft and fluffy pancakes

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add milk and egg.
  3. Stir until smooth.
  4. Pour batter onto hot pan.
  5. Cook until golden on both sides.
